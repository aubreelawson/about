I am a graphic design major and art history minor at the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth. I am excited about getting into the field and working with other designers. I have a love of typography, especially hand drawn letters and look forward to imporoving those skills over the next years.

My decision to part take in an art history minor came from my love of museums and learning and understanding where things come from. This year I was able to participate in curating an exhibition for on of my art history classes. I enjoyed helping set up a gallery and even clean the space up so that it looked presentable. I hope to be able to help in more curations.
